Repurposing a White Paper



Background:

Students were tasked to repurpose a white paper in groups for a company providing evaluation software called eXplorance. In this project, I was designated project manager and created a two-week schedule for teammates to finish the project. The company wishes to make its content appeal to education organizations, without creating new content.


Achievements:

Highest group marks in class. A project management rating average of above 90%


Results:

My group created a presentation using Canva and made a more persuasive content argument. The design of the presentation slides reflected company colors.
