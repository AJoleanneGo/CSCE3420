IMO Document Redesign

Background:

International Maritime Organization created a white paper convincing companies to adopt fuel-saving processes to decrease waste. Prior to the redesign, the white paper was written in standard Microsoft Word preset styles. Students from TECM 3100, Visual Technical Communication, are tasked to redesign ten pages of long-form document.


Achievements:

Full Marks on the assignment


Results:

The document was completely transformed and easier to read. Large blocks of text were broken apart, and important text was placed in sections that make them stand out.
