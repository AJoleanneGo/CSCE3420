Business Case

Background:

A Thompson Packaging, a local jewelry packaging company, requests an internal system investigation to increase productivity and improve the overall efficiency of work. Finances and Sales departments keep track of their expenses through outdated practices like cold calls and spreadsheets, while Manufacturing and Tracking processes are run through an inefficient internal system. Overall, company productivity has significantly decreased, and they are losing long-time clients to inconveniences within the ordering process.

Students are tasked to:

  • Explain the process of Preliminary System Investigations and the importance of a mission statement to an executive through an email.
  • Perform a SWOT Analysis of the company’s situation.
  • Identify factors that affect their IT system.
  • Create a business case explaining the company’s current situation, and suggest a solution that will benefit the company.

Achievements:

Full Marks on the assignment


Results:

Through research and careful analysis of the company’s situation, I was able to recognize the company’s need for a more centralized system and recommended Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Software. The report carefully outlines how this will help the company’s productivity for each affected department, the costs, benefits, and the risks.
